My favorite pdes

Jan 1 2018
mathematics

Navier-Stokes equations:
$$\begin{eqnarray*} \bnabla \bcdot \bm{u} &=& 0, \\ \pd{\bm{u}}{t} + \bm{u} \cdot \bnabla \bm{u} &=& - \bnabla p + \nu \Delta \bm{u}. \end{eqnarray*}$$
and Maxwell equations:
$$\begin{eqnarray*} \bnabla \bcdot \bm{E} &=& \frac{\rho}{\epsilon_0}, \\ \bnabla \bcdot \bm{B} &=& 0, \\ \bnabla \times \bm{E} &=& -\pd{\bm{B}}{t}, \\ \bnabla \times \bm{B} &=& \mu_0 \left( \bm{J} + \varepsilon_0 \pd{\bm{E}}{t} \right). \end{eqnarray*}$$
